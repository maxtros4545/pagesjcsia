Statement of Faith

Statement of Faith

How we see ourselves as believers of Christ Jesus

THE BIBLE

The inspired word of God and the supreme code of conduct for all Christians. (II Tim. 3:16)

THE TRINITY

God the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit manifest themselves as distinct personalities in perfect unity. (LK.3:22)

GOD

The unity of the Godhead eternally existing as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. (Deut.6:4; MK.12:22)

JESUS CHRIST

The true God and true Man who lived a sinless life, died for our sins, and rose again. (Jn.7:38-39)

THE HOLY SPIRIT

The infallible interpreter of God's word, who regenerates and indwells every believer. (Acts 2:37)

MAN

Created in God's image but fallen into sin, needing salvation by grace. (Gen.1:27; Rm.5:12)
