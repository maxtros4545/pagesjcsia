WE ARE DREAM KIDS

Here at Dream Kids we want your child to know they are found and loved by Jesus, to form a relationship with Him, and find family!

Come join us on Sundays at 9:30am and 11:00am!

WHO'S INVOLVED?

Every Sunday morning we provide a safe environment for kids from birth through fifth grade where they can learn about Jesus and connect with each other.

HOW SHOULD I PREPARE MY KIDS?

When you first come in you are welcomed by one of our fantastic volunteers. If it's your first time here, one of our volunteers can help you sign in with your child(ren)'s name, age, birthday, as well as some information about you. After their information is put in, a barcode with your child(ren)'s name will be printed out. You keep the barcode and the name goes on your child. Following service all you have to do is scan your barcode to check out your child and you're good to go!
