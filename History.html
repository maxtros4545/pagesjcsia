History

JCSIA HISTORY

Jesus Christ the Savior International Assemblies (JCSIA) is a full Gospel non- Sectarian church. Where its Doctrines adhere the Assembly of God (AG) world wide. JCSIA is now 33 Years in existence and started its name Asian Christian Charismatic Fellowship(ACCF) under Paul Clark the American Missionary with the Cooperation of Jimmy Swaggart Ministry. The church started with a little Bible Study in Sabarte quezon city and extended to Pangarap Caloocan City. By that time Romeo Analupa as leader of the Bible Study in Pangarap is connected to Jaren Lapasaran a Senior Ptr of ACCF main in Cubao. During this time Jimmy Swaggart missionaries have come to help pentecostal churches in the Philippines, they have conducted crusades and house to house evangelism in different parts of Metro Manila and nearby provinces. These missionaries have done astounding efforts they are able to win many people for Christ. After evangelisn they bought 10 Big lots for churches in Metro Manila and one of the recipients is JCSIA which is located in Bankers Caloocan City. The church started a little member at approximately 50, Ptr Romeo Analupa carried its vision to increase the members of the church therefore he conducted many evangelistic crusades in Quezon City, Caloocan, and Bulacan after eight years of handling ACCF Bankers, he has a new leading of God and he transferred the leadership of the church to his assistant Romeo Anasco.

ACCF TO JCSIA

The Church has now a new leader Rome Anasco, from the Province of Mindanao, Anasco earned his bachelor degree in Christian education at Four Square Bible College. He leads the church for 13 years during his time Mighty Men sprouted these are group of young people in the church who become pillars in prayer and evangelism. Mighty Men is led by Ptr Dong Dotosme the assistant Ptr of Rome Anasco. From this group of young generation of mighty men, the church of JCSIA exploded. By this time Ptr Romeo Anasco is led by the Holy Spirit to change the name of the church from ACCF to JCSIA due to the fact that ACCF churches are dwindling for leadership triumviri. Ptr Jaren the leader of ACCF main goes to USA as a missionary, Bong Valdez and Rick Oasin remain as leader of ACCF and Romeo Anasco change the name of ACCF Bankers to JCSIA as per advice by Ptr Jaren Lapasaran and help of the elders of the local church. After 13 Years of being a Ptr of JCSIA Romeo Anasco transferred his leadership to Derio Dotosme who happens to be his assistant during his leadership, as help by the leadership of Southern Tagalog District Council of the Assembly of God (STDC AG).

CHURCH EXPANSIONS

Lucio "Dong" Dotosme the first assistant Ptr of Romeo Anasco is being led by the Holy Spirit to plant churches in Bulacan, City of San Jose Del Monte. He started in Brangay Tungko the very center of businesses of the city of San Jose. Dong was able to train many leaders to handle churches like Ptr Vic Dedasi, Edgar Tanguna, Yoly Canana and Herman Canan, Robert Dotosme, Edmar Balondo and many others From this time forth the church in Tungko planted another six churches in neighboring Barangays, the church of the Mother church planted 3 Churches in nearby barangay headed by Ptr Derio. Ptr Robert Dotosme was able to plant 12 Churches in neighboring barangay and provinces from city of San Jose to Santa Maria Bulacan to Pampanga. other mighty Men planted churches in North Korea like Ptr Antonio Dotosme, Wally Padero in Dubai, and Liway Poche in Bahrain, and Juben Mendiola in Saudi Arabia, Leovic Ramos in Leyte, and Edo Francisco in Leyte, Bong Sobremonte in Dubai, others are misdionaries in Scotland like Joey Calumba. JCSIA Churches are now 31 in number from Cubao Quezon City, Caloocan City, San Jose City, Santa Maria, Pampanga and to other parts of the world.

ACCF History

History

1985 - ACCF Asian Christian Charismatic Fellowship

ACCF was founded by two American missionaries, Rev. Paul and Jean Khlar as the Lord opened the door for the gospel in the Philippines.

The ministry started as an outreach Bible study of ACCF in Zabarte and was extended to Saguitarius St. in Pangarap Village - residence of Sis. Maria Baris, mother of Ptr. Mark Baris. During that time the worship service was being held in a tent in front of Baris' residence.

With God's mighty moving, the Lord used the Jimmy Swaggart Ministry to donate the current lot of the church and Ptr. Jaren Lapasan of ACCF Main helped commence the ministry of the Lord and appointed Ptr. Romeo Analuap as head pastor of ACCF Caloocan.

1985 - 1986

Donation and construction of the main worship center by the Jimmy Swaggart Ministries

ACCF History & Milestones

Milestones

1985-1986

Ptr. Jaren Lapasan of ACCF Main, Ptr. Mark Baris & Ptr. Romeo Analuap start off the Bible study in Pangarap.

1986-1994

Ptr. Romeo Analuap, with all wisdom and God's mighty moving, led the launch of the ACCF ministry in Bankers Caloocan until he was called by the Lord to a bigger ministry in Novaliches in the year 1994.

1994

Ptr. Romeo Añasco was appointed as head pastor of ACCF, continuing the ministry of the Lord.

I Stand for Christ | I Support One JCSIA

MILESTONES

1996 - JCSIA Jesus Christ the Savior International Assembly

ACCF was reestablished in the year 1996 as Jesus Christ the Saviour International Assembly or JCSIA still under the affiliation of Assemblies of God.

2004 - up to Present

Rev. Derry Dotomse succeeded Ptr. Romeo Añaso as head pastor of JCSIA from 2004 up to present.

The Lord increased the number of the church as His Word spread to the expanse of the church location in Bankers, Caloocan, reaching other barangays in San Jose del Monte City, and thus the Lord added JCSIA Tungko as the first daughter church of JCSIA to the praise of His Glory.

1999

JCSIA Tungko was planted through the work of the Lord in the lives of Rev. Lucio Dotomse and Ptr. Roberto Dotomse.

Milestones - ONE JCSIA

MILESTONES

2006

As the Lord grows the church and increases in numbers, local churches are established throughout different barangays in the cities of San Jose del Monte and Caloocan. The Lord also added pastors and workers to plenty of harvest in His kingdom. With this, a call for unity and one accord was enkindled by head Pastor Derry Dotosme, and so ONE JCSIA is inaugurated.
