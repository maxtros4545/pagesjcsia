In our church we trust in the strength of God's love

In our church, we trust in the strength of God's love, which guides, comforts, and unites us. His love is our foundation, bringing hope in trials, peace in chaos, and joy in faith. Through His grace, we grow stronger, serve others, and walk in His light with unwavering devotion.

We at ONE JCSIA are advancing God's Kingdom thru evangelism, discipleship, sending missionaries and planting churches.

OUR MISSION

OUR MISSION IS TO BE AN AGENT OF THE TRANSFORMATION OF THE PERSON AND THE NATION BY SHARING THE GOOD NEWS OF JESUS CHRIST IN EVERY PLACE AS A RESPONSE TO THE GREAT COMMISSION

Matthew 28:16-20

16 Then the eleven disciples went away into Galilee, into a mountain where Jesus had appointed them.

17 And when they saw him, they worshipped him: but some doubted.

18 And Jesus came and spake unto them, saying, All power is given unto me in heaven and in earth.

19 Go ye therefore, and teach all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost:

20 Teaching them to observe all things whatsoever I have commanded you: and, lo, I am with you always, even unto the end of the world. Amen.

This passage is known as The Great Commission, where Jesus commands His disciples to spread the Gospel to all nations. 🙏
